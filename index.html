Fat Fingers Converter

This tool is designed to help you convert the paragraph you typed with your fingers resting on the wrong keys to something that resembles what you meant to type.

NOTE: This converter assumes you are an amazing typist who hit every incorrect key in the most correct way possible.

Here are some sample sentences you can use:

G99d gr8ef ty8s 8s n9t g98jg weoo;

I nrrf yo hry domr yypinh lrddond.

Oh my towh 5hiw iw qd5uqlly no5 tooe.

Directions

  1. Paste your incorrectly-typed text into the top box.
  2. Click the big green button until you start to see some of the words you meant to type.
  3. Click inside the last box and use your browser's spellchecker and your own editing skills to fix the final problem words/letters.
